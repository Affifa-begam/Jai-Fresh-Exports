About Us

Leading Manufacturers & Exporters of Areca Leaf Plates

For over 12 years, we have been dedicated to producing and exporting eco-friendly Areca leaf plates. Our mission is to promote sustainable living by offering 100% natural, bio-based products. Along with protecting nature, we also empower rural communities by providing fair wages and employment opportunities.

  • Crafted from fresh, naturally fallen Areca palm leaves.
  • Keeps food warm while preserving its natural taste & aroma.
  • 100% eco-friendly, biodegradable & safe for single-use.
  • Durable, leak-proof, lightweight & easy to handle.
