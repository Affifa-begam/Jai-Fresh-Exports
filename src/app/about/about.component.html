Process

Eco-Friendly Bagasse Plates Manufacturing Techniques

Sugarcane residue (bagasse) is collected after juice extraction and dried to remove excess moisture.

The dried bagasse is cleaned and processed into a pulp using high-pressure steam and natural binding agents.

The pulp is molded into plates of various sizes and designs using heat and pressure.

The plates are dried, quality-checked, and packaged for distribution — ready for eco-conscious use.
